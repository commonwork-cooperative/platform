# Roadmap

This is not a pitch deck. The timelines are honest estimates, not commitments. The sequence matters more than the dates.

---

---

## Phase 1A — First Revenue (Months 6 to 12)

**CW Freelance MVP live.**

One platform. One vertical. Workers list services. Clients hire them. 92% reaches the worker. 8% runs the cooperative. That is the entire MVP.

No mobile app. No AI features. No CommonCoin. No Worker Assembly integration in the product itself. Those come after the economic model works with real money and real people.

Success metric: $1M in transaction volume. Not revenue. Transaction volume. That number proves the economic model is real before we build anything else on top of it.

**TruthLayer alpha.**

Browser extension. Chrome only to start. 1,000 beta users. The four-layer truth model (scientific consensus, verifiable facts, contested claims, opinions) implemented for a narrow content category. Grants applied for from Knight Foundation and Mozilla Foundation.

**AnonConnect beta.**

Text only. No images, no video. 5,000 users. CSAM detection infrastructure in place before a single user signs up. This is non-negotiable.

---

## Phase 1B — Proving the Model (Months 12 to 18)

**CW Tasks in one city.**

One city. One service category. Enough workers to prove the matching model. Enough clients to prove there is demand. Not national. Not multiple categories. One thing done well.

**CommonTable pilot.**

One neighborhood. One licensed kitchen. One farm. Two nights a week. One hundred pre-paid household subscriptions sold before the first meal is prepared. The subscriptions fund the kitchen contract. Demand proven before supply committed.

**CommonCoin Phase 1.**

Internal accounting unit only. Members earn CWC through platform participation. Members spend CWC within the ecosystem. No exchange. No external wallets. No speculation. The Algorand infrastructure built and tested at small scale before anything more complex is attempted.

---

## Phase 2 — All Eight Pillars Active (Years 2 to 3)

By this point the economic model is proven, the governance is tested, and the cooperative has real revenue. Every pillar launches in some form. CW Spaces nodes active in multiple cities. CommonWork Open University Level 1 open to the public. First municipal partnership signed. Legal Defense Fund growing toward its $5M target.

Self-funded. No external investor dependency. This is the condition that makes everything else in Phase 3 possible.

---

## Phase 3 and beyond

250,000 worker-members. Political infrastructure active. CommonCoin peer-to-peer between members. Global mobility network operational. The founder transitions to Steward of Mission role at year ten or 50,000 members, whichever comes first.

The details of Phase 3 and beyond depend on what we learn in Phases 1 and 2. Anyone who tells you they know exactly what a cooperative looks like at 250,000 members is guessing. We will update this roadmap as reality provides better information than planning.

---

## What the roadmap is not

This roadmap is not a funding document. There are no valuations, no exit scenarios, and no return projections. CommonWork is a cooperative. The return is patronage dividends distributed to workers in proportion to their contribution. That is the model. It is in the bylaws.

This roadmap is not a promise. It is a direction. The sequence is the commitment, not the dates.
