# CommonWork Cooperative — Open Source Platform

CommonWork is a worker-owned cooperative ecosystem building the world's first unified alternative to the extractive gig economy. Eight interlocking pillars — from gig work to fact-checking to cooperative housing to free education — owned and governed entirely by the workers who build and use them.

## We are looking for builders.

If you are an engineer, designer, AI/ML specialist, blockchain developer, or cooperative attorney who wants their work to mean something — read our founding vision at [commonwork.coop](https://commonwork.coop) and reach out.
